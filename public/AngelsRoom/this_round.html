We're looking for Angels!

In order to facilitate a smooth deleveraging of your favorite coffee roaster, we've decided to use a "smart contract" on the Ethereum Blockchain to raise funds on a per-week basis. Caffeine LaManna will now offer Angel Tokens purchased at our storefront. Each token will come minted with a sale price, an associated amount of roasted & shipped whole coffee beans, and a share of the next round's profits once it executes.

At the very least, you get the same fresh bean deal you'd get otherwise and you could stock up for the year if you wanted! But if all goes well, Caffeine LaManna mints another round of tokens. You'll get an email to let you in on the details of the new round in case you want in again, or want to see your possible share should the new round execute.

When the new round is executed, the wallet associated with the token receives its designated share and an email is sent to the Angel holding the tokens to let them know the round is complete. And that's it!
